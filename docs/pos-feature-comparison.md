**Notes:**
- **Square**: Best for small to medium businesses, strong free tier
- **Shopify POS**: Best for e-commerce integration and omnichannel retail
- **Lightspeed**: Versatile, strong in both retail and restaurant sectors
- **Vend**: Focused on retail, strong inventory management
- **Toast POS**: Restaurant-specific, comprehensive hospitality features
- **Clover**: Versatile with proprietary hardware ecosystem

This comparison should help you understand the competitive landscape and identify features that would make your Elavon-based POS SAAS solution competitive in the market.
